## Logo

**Wordmark:** "Call It a Day" in Plus Jakarta Sans, medium weight.

**App icon / favicon:** Indigo circle with white checkmark — `#4F46E5` fill.

---

## Tone

Quiet, earned, honest. The app doesn't cheer — it confirms. The moment you hit your hours should feel like a nod, not a party.
